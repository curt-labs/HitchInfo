About HitchInfo.com

Your one-stop trailer hitch and towing lifestyle guide!

What you’ll find here is extensive information and practical advice on trailer hitches and towing - everything from how to select & install the proper hitch for your vehicle, to learning how to back up your trailer in a tight spot. You’ll also get connected to a community of like-minded recreational enthusiasts who hitch up their boats, trailers, and cargo carriers and tow away! If you don’t find what you’re looking for, or have a suggestion please let us know!

If you're new to towing, start with Learn the Basics - easy to navigate information on what you need to know before you hitch up your boat, camper, trailer, or recreational vehicle, and tow safely to your destination. Then browse our articles and discover tips on just about every topic you can imagine, including Safe Towing and Trailer Hitch Installation Tips. When you encounter unfamiliar towing terms – especially the alphabet soup of acronyms that are common in the automotive industry – check out our glossary of terms for clarification.

If you prefer "at a glance" information - take a look at the checklists & quick reference section. And last but not least the FAQ section covers a number of common trailer hitch and towing related questions.
Get help from The Hitch Guys™

Even the pros get stumped sometimes and need help. Talk to The Hitch Guys™ via our LIVE CHAT feature or email your questions, and someone will get back with you right away. Read The Hitch Guys™ Blog regularly for practical towing advice and lively discussion from the recreational towing industry's top experts.

Find hitch installation instructions

This site has detailed, up to date installation instructions for hundreds of CURT trailer hitch part numbers, including receiver hitches, 5th wheel and gooseneck hitches. If you know the part number for your hitch, enter it on the search page. If your part number is not listed then your hitch is most likely NOT a CURT hitch. Call your hitch dealer and ask for the latest installation instructions for your vehicle. For general installation information, read the Top Ten Hitch Installation Tips.

If you don’t know your part number, you can still search for CURT hitch installation instructions by your vehicle's year/make/model. After you enter your vehicle information, the results page shows the available hitches for your vehicle, and has links to their corresponding installation instructions.
